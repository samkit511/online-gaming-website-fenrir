# Online Gaming Website

## Project Objective

Build a modern, responsive online gaming platform that allows users to discover, access, and participate in multiplayer and single-player gaming experiences through a unified web interface.

The platform should provide user account management, game discovery, leaderboards, tournaments, social interaction features, and premium membership capabilities while maintaining strong usability, security, and performance standards.

---

# Client Background

PixelForge Interactive is a digital gaming and entertainment company focused on developing online gaming platforms and player communities. The organization supports casual and competitive gaming experiences through multiplayer systems, tournaments, leaderboards, and social interaction features designed for a global audience.
