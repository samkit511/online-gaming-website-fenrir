Project Name: Online Gaming Website

Overview:
Develop a modern online gaming website that enables users to discover, access, and participate in multiplayer and single-player gaming experiences through a unified platform. The website should provide user account management, leaderboards, tournaments, social interaction features, and premium membership options while maintaining a responsive, secure, and engaging user experience across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Target Audience:
- Casual gamers
- Competitive gamers
- Esports enthusiasts
- Teenagers and adults
- Online gaming communities

Key Features:
- User registration and login
- User profiles and achievements
- Multiplayer matchmaking
- Game catalog and search
- Leaderboards and rankings
- Tournament management
- Friends and chat system
- Premium memberships
- In-game purchases
- Mobile responsive design

Business Goal:
Create a scalable online gaming platform that increases user engagement, builds an active gaming community, and generates revenue through premium memberships, in-platform purchases, and sponsored gaming events.
